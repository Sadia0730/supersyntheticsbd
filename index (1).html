About

Super Synthetics Ltd. is one of the foremost and leading polyester yarn manufacturers in Bangladesh.

The factory is located in Beyezid Bustami Road,Nasirabad Industrial Area, Chittagong. Registered Corporate Office address is T.K. Group, 83 Khatungonj,Chittagong -4000. It began its journey as a polyester yarn producer with the vision to meet the local market demand on 10th September 2001. The company was launched by enterprising personalities Mr. Md.Abu Tayab, Mr. Md.Abul Kalam and Mr.Md.Abu Bakar with a primary aim of becoming a major name among the polyester yarn manufacturers in Bangladesh.We consider our manpower, the strongest asset. We have a professional team expertise in its department & have set up specific departments to handle the Purchase, Sales, Quality Control, Logistics & Documentation.
